## Recall Relevance Rubric (Locked for v0.2)

Use this rubric for every scored recall result in baseline and pilot runs.

- **5**: Direct answer to query intent; correct file/section anchor; no extra triage needed.
- **4**: Mostly correct and useful; minor extra triage (one follow-up lookup).
- **3**: Partially relevant; contributes context but does not directly answer query.
- **2**: Weak relevance; generic or noisy result requiring significant manual filtering.
- **1**: Irrelevant or misleading for the asked query.
